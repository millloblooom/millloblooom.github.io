Mission Statement

The goal of Apimba is to collect knowledge and make it accessible. Knowledge not only needs to be available to anyone who is interested, it also has to be stripped of history, conjecture and arcane terminology. The remainder is knowledge in its purest form, and would be the same in any language or country (or planet!). It is still a lot of information, but written records only go back a couple thousand years, which is a blink of an eye, and should be easily accumulated and sorted eventually.

The universe might seem infinitely complex, but there are strict limits on what is and is not possible. This means that the amount of knowledge is finite, and it is just a matter of time and effort until it is all discovered. Theories and fancies are infinite, realities are not, and can thus be conquered.

Apimba is going to do just that.
